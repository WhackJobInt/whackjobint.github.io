Catharsis Reborn

Catharsis Reborn is a total conversion mod for Postal III that aims to turn one of the worst games ever into an actually good game. To do this, Catharsis Reborn boasts an entirely new story, new weapons, a variety of new content and mechanics, and a LOT of fixes and improvements!
